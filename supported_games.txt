BETHINI PIE — SUPPORTED GAMES
==============================

Full Support (all settings available):
  - The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Special Edition (SE)
  - The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Anniversary Edition (AE)
  - The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Legendary Edition (LE)
  - The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion Remastered
  - Starfield
  - Fallout 4
  - Fallout: New Vegas
  - Fallout 3

Partial Support (core settings only):
  - Morrowind (basic INI editing)

Not Supported (different engine):
  - The Elder Scrolls Online
  - Fallout 76
  - Fallout Shelter

Notes:
  - Always close Mod Organizer 2 or Vortex before launching BethINI Pie
  - For Skyrim VR, use the standard Skyrim SE profile
  - Starfield requires the StarfieldPrefs.ini file to be present
